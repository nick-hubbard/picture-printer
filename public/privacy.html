Photo Printer

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

What Photo Printer Does

Photo Printer helps you choose photos, arrange them on printable pages, and print or download the prepared pages from your browser.

Photos You Upload

Photos selected from your device are used only to create the print preview and prepared page files you request. Hosted deployments may temporarily store uploaded files and prepared pages while processing your request.

Google Photos Access

If you connect Google Photos, Photo Printer uses the Google Photos Picker API so you can explicitly choose which photos to share with the app. The app requests the photospicker.mediaitems.readonly permission only to create picker sessions, read selected picker items, and download the specific photos you selected for printing.

Photo Printer does not request access to your full Google Photos library, does not modify or delete your Google Photos, and does not sell or share Google user data for advertising.

Storage And Retention

This version stores Google Photos connection tokens and selected photo files in temporary application storage. On hosted deployments, that storage may be cleared after deployments, cold starts, or platform cleanup. Prepared files are intended for immediate printing or download.

Third Parties

The app uses Google OAuth and the Google Photos Picker API when you connect Google Photos. Hosted deployments may run on Vercel infrastructure.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app operator listed on the Google OAuth consent screen.
